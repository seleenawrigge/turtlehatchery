The Hikkaduwa Turtle Sanctuary

The sea turtle hatchery in Sri Lanka works to safeguard and save the sea turtles who visit the island's golden coasts, from treating sick or injured turtles to providing a secure breeding area. The Sea Turtle Hatchery and Rescue Center aims to preserve some of the world's endangered turtle species, it is located a few kilometers north of the main Hikkaduwa town. The southern shore of the island is home to several turtle hatcheries, including this one. It began as a community initiative to help safeguard the southern coast's biodiversity, but it has since developed into a full-fledged hatchery and rescue facility with devoted personnel that work around the clock to study, look after, and safeguard various turtle species.
